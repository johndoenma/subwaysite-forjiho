NUTRITION

We think every day is an opportunity to improve our ingredients where ever and whenever we can. We locally source our vegetables whenever possible through a network of growers around the country - many are family farmers. Our goal is to serve the highest quality food safely. There are no colors from artificial sources in our roast beef, turkey, rotisserie-style chicken, chicken strips, oven roasted chicken, and meatballs. Our rotisserie-style chicken and chicken strips are from chicken raised without antibiotics.
